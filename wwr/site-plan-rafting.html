Overview

Purpose

We here at Whitewater Rafting strive to provide a high quality rafting experience at an affordable cost!

Audience

Who are the target customers? Those who enjoy going whitewater rafting or are avid thrill seekers who may be looking for more affordible services. What do they expect to find by them accessing the website? They will find information on our many services that we offer as well as our availability dates and price ranges. How will my audience usually access my site? Most will use their desktops or laptops to look at our rafting dates, or make appointments. But some will use their phones to do the same things.
